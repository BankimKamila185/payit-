Why this console exists. The payment app and its fraud engine have no authority over an account at this bank — they can only request. When the engine is confident, it places a provisional block and files the case here. This desk rules on the bank's own evidence (recent fan-in, account age, independent customer reports) and will CLEAR & unblock an account the engine got wrong. The engine's own report is not counted as evidence — a block cannot justify itself.

Reversals follow the same principle (ISO 20022 camt.056 request → camt.029 resolution): a reversal needs a law-enforcement reference (India: 1930 / CFCFRMS), otherwise the bank can only hold a lien — reject code RQDA. Funds already moved on → AM04.
